Internal LCA Study

An internal LCA conducted for the process of product development / improvement, strategic planning, or capital investments when necessary. The scope depends on the product, but this is typically conducted analyzing the cradle to grave life cycle of the product.

Timeframe: 8-16 weeks, depending on data quality and availability

Comprehensive Time Series Application

An application developed to be able to create a large variety of time series models based upon time series data submitted by the user. So far this app works on annual, quarterly, monthly, and daily data.

Some of the models which can be developed include Summary models, Basic Time Series Models, Linear Regression Models, Exponential Smoothing models, and both seasonal and non-seasonal ARIMA models. The application also provides model statistics – which can be used for judging the best model – as well as the forecasted values of each model of your choice.
