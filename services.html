Life Cycle Assessment

Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) is the process of analyzing a product's entire life cycle, from the raw material extraction and acquisition through manufacture, use, and end of life. There are many benefits of conducting an LCA, including being a useful tool for product development and improvement, strategic planning, or a background study for the creation of promotional materials.

We offer the following life cycle assessment services to help you determine the full environmental and human health impact of your products as well as build capacity within your organization.

Internal LCA Study

An internal LCA conducted for the process of product development / improvement, strategic planning, or capital investments when necessary. The scope depends on the product, but this is typically conducted analyzing the cradle to grave life cycle of the product.

Timeframe: 8-16 weeks, depending on data quality and availability
